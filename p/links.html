Campaign for Southern Equality - non-profit based in Asheville, NC promoting LGBTQ equality across Southern states.
  • Website: southernequality.org
  • Trans in the South: A Directory of Trans-Affirming Health & Legal Service Providers - a directory of more than 400 Southern health service providers who are trans-affirming. Link: southernequality.org/resources/transinthesouth
  • Instagram: @southernequality
  • Twitter: @SouthernEqual
  • Linktree: linktr.ee/southernequality
Crossport (Cincinatti) - a not-for-profit* social support organization serving transgender individuals in the Greater Cincinnati and surrounding area, including partners, families, friends, and other allies.
  • Website: crossport.org
  • Instagram: @crossportcincy
Queer Kentucky - an LGBTQ+ nonprofit publication. I especially like Thinking Queerly.
  • Instagram: @queerkentucky
  • Twitter: @queerkentucky
  • Linktree: linktr.ee/queerkentucky
Fairness Campaign - LGBTQ+ advocacy organization promoting civil rights legislation.
